Q: What is the purpose of the doctype declaration?
A: To allow the browser to recognize the file as belonging to
    a particular version of HTML.

Q: What is the HTML element?
A: <!DOCTYPE html>

Q: What is the purpose of the head element?
A: To contain important internal information that the website 
    needs to display correctly, but shoudn't be seen by the user. 

Q: What is the purpose of the body element?
A: To contain the text which will be seen on the webpage by 
    the user.
